About Us

Founder and ceo
Foggy Nelson

Product manager and
lead web designer
Brett Mahoney

Foggy Nelson founded True Beard in 2012 after he decided to grow a beard and saw how hard it was to get good quality beard grooming products. Most products he could buy were really low quality, and the ones that were higher quality were way too expensive. So after doing some research he decided to start his own beard care product company and named it True Beard. Shortly after, he met Brett Mahoney who was one of his first customers. Foggy found out that Brett was a web developer so he asked him to come work for him as a web designer and product manager. They have been coworkers and best friends ever since and they are planning on expanding the team due to the company's growth.
